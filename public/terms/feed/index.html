Feed Guidelines

The Crooms Bell Schedule website (referred to as the "Service") has a Feed feature, which displays curated content submitted by students. Guidelines were developed to ensure that any submitted content would be appropriate for marketing.

Usage of the Feed feature

You may use the Feed to catch up on some curated news, and/or submit your own topics to appear in the feed. All content submitted must follow the guidelines below and the Terms of Service.

Content Restrictions

The following content is prohibited from being submitted to the Feed:

  • Content that dehumanizes an individual, group, race or ethnicity. This includes any hate language, slurs, dirty jokes or any content released without the owner's permission.
  • Content that violates copyright laws. This includes any and all works that you do not have permission to use.
  • Content that can be classified as obscene, including content with excessive profanity, obscene gestures, and pornographic content.

Also, content must abide by the Acceptable Content Guidelines.

Policy of Truth

All content must be of true statement. No content may be made of false information. Any content that contains false information will be concidered a violation of the guidelines.

Defining Content

Content is any text, images, video, or audio that is directly inserted, embedded, or linked in or on the "Service". All content must follow the guidelines above. For more information about what defines content that violates the guidelines, please view the Acceptable Content Guidelines.

Spam and Scams

Spamming requests or content is prohibited. In addition, links to scams are strictly prohibited.

Surveys

You are not allowed to add links to surveys or questionaires.

Violations to the Guidelines

If a user violates the Guidelines above, their access to certain features will be temporarily restricted or blocked. Additional penalties may be imposed if the content also violated the Acceptable Content Guidelines or the Terms of Service.
