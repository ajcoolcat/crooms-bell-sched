Terms of Service

We believe in equal and safe access.

That's what our Terms of Service aims to do. If you ever feel like you're excluded and want to recommend a change, please email our manager (Andrew) so we can work on it.

What you can do.

Regardless of whatever service you're using, there are basic rights we will provide to all users. Please note that these rights may be affected if you violate the Terms of Service.

  • Access the services without interruption.
  • Post to and interact with the services.
  • Engage in meaningful and entertaining discussions.

What you can do if it's okay.

There are certain things that may be offensive or disturbing and should get approval from our administration before being done on our services. Please note that these rights may be terminated if you violate the Terms of Service.

  • Talk about sensitive political topics.
  • Talk about controversial topics.
  • Talking about teachers' unique habits.
    • Please don't talk about the really weird things though.

What you can't do.

These things can get you restricted, suspended, or banned from Crooms Bell Schedule services.

  • Not showing basic kindness and respect towards others, regardless of anyone's background.
  • Advertising without providing payments to the Crooms Bell Schedule administration.
    • If you are interested in a custom advertising spot, contact Andrew.
  • Using slurs.
    • It is not permitted to call people slurs in any fashion.
  • Talking about personal sexual actions.
  • Spamming or attempting to disable our servers by DDoSing them.

Consequences for violating our Terms.

We will take action against violations of our Terms the first time we see it. We also accept anonymous tips.

First Offense
This will only be a warning, unless the act is too severe. If so, we may just skip to Second or Third Offense.
Second Offense
This will be a temporary account suspension, usually for only a day, but may be longer depending on the offense.
Third Offense
This will be an extended account suspension or termination. If administration deems it necessary, there may be system-wide bans or lockdowns.
