Acceptable Content Guidelines

The Crooms Bell Schedule website (referred to as the "Service") allows users to submit content to certain locations to be featured on the website. The "Author" (Andrew Jennings) has enforced certain guidelines to ensure the safety and comfort of all users. These guidelines exist as an extention to the Terms of Service.

Defining Content

Content is any text, images, video, or audio that is directly inserted, embedded, or linked in or on the "Service". All content must follow the guidelines below.

Content Restrictions

The following content is prohibited from being used within the "Service":

  • Content that dehumanizes an individual, group, race or ethnicity. This includes any hate language, slurs, dirty jokes or any content released without the owner's permission.
  • Content that violates copyright laws. This includes any and all works that you do not have permission to use.
    • An exemption will be granted for fair use.
  • Content that may violate COPPA. COPPA is a child protection act that all website operators must follow. No data about a child can be requested from them without parental consent.
  • Content that can be classified as obscene, including content with excessive profanity, obscene gestures, and pornographic content.
  • Content that can be classified as sexual. This includes any recordings, photos or enticing wording for a link.
  • Content that promotes illicit items, such as alchohol or drugs.
  • Content that promotes self-harm, including suicide.
  • Content that promotes smoking.
  • Content that promotes, depicts, or refrences violence, murder, or felonies.
    • An exception will be granted to content that is used in an educational manner (e.g. An article that describes a recent criminal trend).
  • Content that depicts or debates information referring to dating.
  • Content that promotes or depicts teen violence or juvenile delinquents.
    • An exception will be granted to content that is used in an educational manner (e.g. A novel that describes the ill effects of violence in teens).

Spam and Scams

Spamming requests or content is prohibited. In addition, links to scams will not be allowed.

Violations to the Guidelines

If a user violates the Acceptable Content Guidelines, the content will be removed, and their access to certain features will be temporarily restricted or blocked. However, if a user violates any guidelines above in red, their access to all extra features will be terminated.
